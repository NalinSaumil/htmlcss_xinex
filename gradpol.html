Grading Policy

In our school, academic honesty is taken very seroiusly. We have very strict grading policy. A 90 or above is an "A". An 80 or above and below 90 is a "B". A 70 or above and below 80 is a "C". A below 70 is an "F". Late grades are automattically deducted to 70. Academic dishonesty, also know as cheating, will not be tolerated at all and will result in an automatic "F" and an in-school suspension will be issued immediately.
